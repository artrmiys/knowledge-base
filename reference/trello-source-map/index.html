    Integration Status¶

    • Interior Trims text rules are folded into the trim topic pages: Base, Crown, and Door and Window Trim.
    • Important Changes text-only cards are summarized on Important Changes and copied into the closest framing/sheathing/QA topic pages when the rule belongs there.
    • Private/tooling link cards stay raw-only.
