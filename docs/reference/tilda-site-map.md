## Walls Page Summary

Imported into:

- [Exterior Walls](../work/vertical/walls/exterior.md)
- [Unit / Interior Walls](../work/vertical/walls/unit.md)
- [Sill Plates](../work/vertical/walls/sill-plates.md)
- [Box Sheathing](../work/vertical/sheathing/box-sheathing.md)

Key points:

- Track wall height by basement, first floor, second floor, loft/attic, chimney,
  and dormers.
- Count Sill Plates and Shelf Plates with visible notes.
- For Sill Plates under KD Plates, note the two lower boards and P.T. lower
  plate.
- Split basement/first/second/attic walls by 2x4, 2x6, 2x8, exterior, furring,
  flat furring, knee, chimney, dormer, garage, and interior conditions.
- Put Studs PreCut, corners, corner studs, extra plates, and garage door quantity
  where applicable.
- Check hidden box sheathing in sections.

## Gables Page Summary

Imported into:

- [Gable Walls](../work/vertical/walls/gable.md)
- [Gable Sheathing](../work/vertical/sheathing/gable.md)

Key points:

- Track exterior, interior, porch, truss, and dormer gables.
- Gables can be 2x4 or 2x6.
- Track sheathing area.
- For truss gables, remove plates and studs from the left/output side.
- Dormers should be recorded with wall scope.
- Gable stud height should be visible on the left/output side.
