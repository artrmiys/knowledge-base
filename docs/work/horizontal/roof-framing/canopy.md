# Canopy

## Count

- Canopy framing, soffit sheathing, exterior-grade gypsum/Denseglas, posts,
  beams, and attachments.

## Check

- Canopies and porch soffits may need 5/8" exterior-grade gypsum soffit sheathing.
- RCP and architectural sheets often show canopy soffit framing.
- Metal stud joists may be required at gazebo/canopy conditions.
