# Dormer

## Count

- Dormer walls, roof framing, sheathing, trims, and openings as separate scope
  when shown.

## Check

- Dormers often combine wall, roof, opening, and trim rules.
- Verify exterior sheathing/WRB and FRT rules.
- Do not hide dormer materials inside generic roof lines if pricing needs them
  separate.
