# Roof Types

## Что считать

- Asphalt, metal, flat roof и alternate roof systems — только когда in scope.
- Cover boards, rigid insulation, XPS, glass mat, and Densedeck for flat roofs.

## Проверить

- Plans могут показывать asphalt shingles и standing seam metal alternates.
- Flat roof trusses often need Densedeck/cover board over the same area as rigid
  insulation.
- Piggy trusses require sleeper review.
